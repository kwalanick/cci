MediPack® outpatient care package


A well planned safari to the wilds of Kenya can be the trip of a lifetime! An important part of that planning is for your health. Your continued health is very important in making the most of your holiday. Most visitors to Kenya purchase medical and travel insurance before arriving in Kenya. However the quality of medical care in Kenya varies greatly and medical costs for foreigners is often very high. Although insured the up front costs for treatment or consultation can be substantial and reimbursement from your insurance provider can be slow in coming and usually only covers a portion of your expenses. That’s where we make a significant difference. MediPack® International LTD, a Canadian company working only with qualified Kenyan medical service partners, is pleased to announce a new and one of a kind outpatient health care package that adds convenience and security to your travels while in Kenya.

Travel Safety Tips

Food safety:

Drink bottled water only and avoid foods like ice cream treats and snacks sold by street vendors. It's generally safe when foods are thoroughly cooked and still piping hot when served.
